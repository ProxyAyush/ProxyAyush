### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a **Software Engineer** and **Doctor** passionate about the intersection of healthcare, gaming, and artificial intelligence. I specialize in building high-performance Android applications and leveraging Generative AI to solve complex problems. My work ranges from immersive game engines to AI-driven medical assistants.

*   🔭 I’m currently building **[Lumixo](https://github.com/ProxyAyush/Lumixo)**, a Solarpunk block puzzle adventure with a custom engine.
*   🧠 I’m researching **Generative AI** workflows for automated asset creation.
*   🌱 I’m exploring **Computer Vision** for biomedical waste sorting.
